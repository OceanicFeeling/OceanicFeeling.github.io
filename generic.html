Hi, my name is Marta

My own journey with acupuncture started over 8 years ago, when, still new to the Country , I started experiencing panic attacks, irregular eating patterns and migraines. I looked for help through therapists, but nothing seemed to help the way I felt I needed - until one day I heard of Five Elements acupuncture and thought to myself - “alright then, might as well try”! What I experienced was the most profound, lasting shift within me that I ever could have hoped for. The panic attacks stopped, the migraines came to an end, and every part of my life started feeling more grounded and more in balance.

Now, after completing my training under renowned fertility-expert and Acupuncture Master Gerad Kite, I am committed to supporting people in their life journeys through the acknowledgment that we are spiritual beings in physical form – a manifestation of Nature. As such, I work with the needles to understand what is the root cause of my patients’ imbalance, to bring them back in alignment with themselves, their purpose, and their innate potential at a full life-experience.
