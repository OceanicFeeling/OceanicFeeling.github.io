Acupuncture is a spiritual tradition and an empowering system of preventative Chinese medicine that dates back thousands of years. It addresses the needs of people at the level of the body, mind and spirit. It is one of the safest medical practices on offer in the UK today and is used by thousands of people as an effective way of improving their health. It facilitates self-healing through the understanding that our natural state is that of balance: we have within us the capacity to sleep easily and feel rested, to eat and then digest that food well, to grieve and then move on, to open ourselves up to others and not lose ourselves, to stand our ground without biting someone's head off! We'll do this by acknowledging that Nature follows five "movements", which are mirrored in the seasons (and in you!): Spring, Summer, Harvest, Autumn and Winter. These are also known as the Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal and Water elements. When the system goes out of balance, all sorts of diseases will come up: my job as your acupuncturist is to find out the root cause of your malaise, and then support you back to your full potential, so that you can live your best, fullest, most harmonious life, restoring health across the board. This is why acupuncture can treat a wide range of ailments: on a physical level, it can help with chronic conditions such as Crohn's disease, IBS, arthritis, migraines - as well as fertility issues, hormonal imbalances, insomnia and “unexplained illnesses”. On a mental level it has been proven to be highly beneficial for individuals who suffer from depression, anxiety, stress, burnout, grief. On a spiritual level, it supports people who feel stuck in their lives, disconnected, who are going through a period of transition or who feel lost in their purpose.
